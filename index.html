About Civil Engineering Study Hub

As passionate civil engineer students, we created Civil Engineering Study Hub or CESH to provide comprehensive materials that will help aspiring engineers excel in their academic and professional journeys, making complex concepts simple and accessible.

The path to become an engineer is not an easy one, therefore, we hope that this site will pave the road to our fellow aspirants, or to those interested.
